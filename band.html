Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is pop rock band from Las Vegas, Nevada.There first song that sparked popularity was "It's Time" in 2012.Some of thre other most popular songs include "Radioactive", "Demons",and "Thunder".Imagine Dragons has won three American Music Awards, nine Billboard Music Awards, one Grammy Award, one MTV Video Music Award and one World Music Award. Imagine Dragons has sold 12 million allbums and 35 million singles worldwide.
